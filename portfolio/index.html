    • A Brief Introduction

    Welcome to my Official Website! As you may know, I work as a Cartoonist, Comic Book Artist & Animation Concept Artist. I studied Digital Media at Seminole State College of Florida, and have acquired certain level of experience in Adobe Photoshop, MediBang Paint, GIMP and Blender 3D, among other apps. My main focus when drawing or scripting cartoons, short animations or comics is to bring constructive solutions to many of modern-day issues, but at the same time, keep the tone family-friendly. Such is the purpose of many of my creator-owned characters, such as Lud the Aurochs.

    Over the years, I have acquired the rule of strictly sticking to deadlines, but avoid rushing at the same time. Often, fast-paced work will not benefit me or my clients in the long term, so I always do everything possible to keep clear deadlines for finishing projects, yet following every single procedure carefully and in order of importance. This is especially important for me, since my preference is to work by myself, developing a few versions of a single Concept and allowing the client to choose which will be used.
