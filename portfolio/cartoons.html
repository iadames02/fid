    • The Cartoon Gallery

    This is my cartoon gallery, which will include several of both my creator-owned characters dating to 2017, and some fictional characters of the Golden Age of Animation (between 1928 and 1959). Most of the characters here are based on the rubber-hose art style used by Walt Disney's Silly Symphonies and Van Beuren's Aesop Fables.
