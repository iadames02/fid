    • The Cartoon Megastars of Van Beuren

    A concept sketch of three cartoon characters from the now-defunct animation studios of Van Beuren, which were famous during the Golden Age of Animation in the 1930's. These are Cubby the Bear, Molly-Moo Cow and Felix the Cat, originally drawn by Pat Sullivan's Commonwealth Pictures. Sketch done in MediBang Paint c. 2022
